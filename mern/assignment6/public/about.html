Our Story

Founded in 2024, Laundry Mart has grown from a local washhouse into a leading tech-enabled laundry service provider. We combine environment-friendly detergents, advanced washing machines, and expert garment care staff to deliver the cleanest and freshest clothes.

Our goal is to give you back your free time. Instead of spending weekends sorting clothes and waiting for dry cycles, you can schedule a pickup with us in less than a minute and let us do the rest.

Why Choose Us?

  • Eco-Friendly Methods: We utilize biodegradable detergents that are gentle on garments and the planet.
  • Express Delivery: Get your dry cleaning and standard laundry back in as fast as 24 hours.
  • Dedicated Care: We sort garments individually and follow all specific fabric care label instructions.
